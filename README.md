# What is ATHENA?

ATHENA is an **Evidence-Based Decision Intelligence Operating System (EDIOS)** built to help investors make **better decisions**, not simply execute more trades.

Unlike traditional trading software that focuses on buy/sell signals, ATHENA combines:

- 📊 Market Intelligence
- 📈 Quantitative Research
- 🎯 Probability Analysis
- 🤖 Explainable AI
- 🛡 Risk Management
- 📚 Knowledge Management
- 🔬 Continuous Learning

ATHENA is being built as a **professional-grade research and decision platform** that evolves through evidence, experimentation, and disciplined engineering.

- Explain Every Decision
- Learn Continuously
- AI Assists — Humans Decide

---

# What Makes ATHENA Different?

ATHENA does **not** ask:

> Which stock should I buy?

ATHENA asks:

- Should I trade today?
- Why?
- What evidence supports this?
- What is the probability of success?
- What is the downside?
- What can we learn afterwards?
